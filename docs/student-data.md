Student Data Inventory
This document details the specific data categories and attributes stored within the student management system.

1. Profile & Identity Data
Full Name: Legal identity of the learner (e.g., Ryan Phillips).

Email Address: Primary communication and account identifier.

Student ID: Unique system-generated hexadecimal identifier.

Initials/Avatar: Visual representation for quick identification (e.g., "RP").

Identification Documents: Digital records of ID verification status (e.g., "verified").

2. Academic Progress & Participation
Course Enrollment: The specific program title (e.g., Professional Electrical Course) and enrollment date.

Module Completion: A granular tracking of participation across modules (M1–M10), including status (Completed, In Progress, Not Started).

Activity Metrics: Quantitative tracking of completed courses (0/2), modules (0/10), and individual activities (0/80).

Overall Progress: A high-level percentage reflecting total program completion.

Assessments: Performance data including passed counts, items pending review, and those needing resubmission.

3. Financial & Legal Records
Payment Tracking: Total course fees (£3,000), amount paid (£900), and outstanding balance (£2,100).

Payment Settings: Current method (Bank Transfer) and payment structure (One Time).

Financial Timeline: Visual and chronological log of deposits and scheduled payments.

Legal Compliance: Status of essential documents such as the Enrollment Agreement and Payment Authorization.

4. Support & Engagement
Support Tickets: Interaction history and status for student inquiries (visible via the "Tickets" tab).

Activity History: A time-stamped audit log of system events, such as email triggers and enrollment creation.

Communication State: Logs of automated system emails sent to the student.
